Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Badulla city located?

Badulla is located in the Uva Province of Sri Lanka, surrounded by mountains and tea estates.

What are the best places to visit in Badulla?

Dunhinda Waterfall, Muthiyangana Temple, Demodara Loop, and Ella are popular attractions.

What is the best time to visit Badulla?

December to April offers pleasant weather for sightseeing.

How can I reach Badulla?

You can reach Badulla by train, bus, or private vehicle.
